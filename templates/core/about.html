Dr. Bhubon Chatterji

Director & Lead Specialist

With more than 20 years in the field, Dr. Chatterji has developed a unique approach that prioritizes long-term restoration over short-term relief. His philosophy is rooted in the belief that every patient deserves a precise, evidence-based treatment plan tailored to their specific needs.

Having trained extensively in advanced manual therapy, sports rehabilitation, and spine care, Dr. Chatterji brings a rare combination of clinical depth and empathetic care to every patient interaction. His pioneering work in tele-rehabilitation has made world-class physiotherapy accessible to patients globally.
